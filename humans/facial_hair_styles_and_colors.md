# Facial Hair Styles & Colors

Valid facial hair styles:

```json
[
  "beard_ducktail_01",
  "beard_dutch_01",
  "beard_klingon_01",
  "full_ducktail_01",
  "full_garibaldi_01"
  "full_generic_01",
  "mustache_english_01",
  "mustache_painters_01",
  "mustache_walrus_01",
]
```

| beard_klingon         | beard_klingon_01      |

| full_garibaldi        | full_garibaldi_01     |
